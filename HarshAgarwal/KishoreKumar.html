SaReGaMa CARVAAN
The Provider of ultimate soothe and tranquility



With over 80+ dedicated music stations, SaReGaMa CARVAAN lets us listen to one of the greatest vocalists, instrumentalists and players ever born over the Indian subcontinent. Taking birth in Generation Z which have been raised with screens, has never squeezed out my zeal to relate to these greats. All the pains, joys, sorrows and grieves in their voices travel down the time to reach us and induce an essence of calmness. I'd supplement the 'OLD IS GOLD' quotation by adding that 'OLD IS MUCH MORE BEAUTIFUL THAN WHAT YOU'VE BEEN TOLD'.
